Normal India

Make India Normal Together

While your loved ones are suffering with Carona (COVID) and fighting for life, it is a really painful and heart breaking experience to go in search of bed, plasma, or oxygen. Right information at the right time can help save a life. So let us work together as one family to bring India back to normal by sharing information the right way.

YOU Can Help Save Lives
  • Report - Do you have latest information about availability of beds, plasma, vaccine, etc.? You can report in real-time to keep everyone informed
  • Support - If you find a report to be helpful or not let the world know by clicking or
  • Share - You can inspire others to join you in this effort. Because together we all can save more lives
  • Get Notified - Are you looking for beds, plasma, vaccine, or oxygen? Use 'Notify Me' to stay informed
